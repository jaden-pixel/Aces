The Hand

Aces District holds a hard corner position at Weber Road and Normantown Road in Romeoville — directly off I‑355, among the busiest retail crossroads in the southwest Chicago suburbs.

The development is built around a single idea: give drivers a reason to stop. Aces Dispensary anchors the lineup alongside a steakhouse, cigar lounge, and open retail bays, while seven Tesla Supercharging stalls turn every visit into a charging stop.

Two freestanding buildings, 10,500 SF each, sit across two tracts totaling 3.55 acres — built for tenants who want visibility, parking, and traffic that's already there.
